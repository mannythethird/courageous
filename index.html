The Mission Is Moving

We’re winning when men are finding real faith and taking real steps toward Jesus.

  • New men are stepping into Courageous every month.
  • Men under 40 are actively engaging, not just attending.
  • Every campus can point to fresh stories of life change.
  • The conversation about men is happening outside of church walls (gyms, homes, workplaces).

Leaders Are Multiplying

We’re winning when men aren’t waiting for permission—they’re leading with courage.

  • Table leaders are being developed into coaches; coaches are becoming head coaches.
  • Every man knows who he’s raising up behind him.
  • Leadership huddles are full and relationally strong.
  • Pastors aren’t managing; they’re mentoring.

Men Are Transforming

We’re winning when men are taking what happens in a group and living it everywhere else.

  • You can hear language shifts — from “me” to “we.”
  • Marriages are being restored, Fathers are being present, and purpose is being discovered.
  • Men are practicing the Spiritual Practices: Sabbath, Prayer, Bible Reading, Fasting, and authentic community.
  • Stories of healing and breakthrough are normal.
How We Measure It
Transformation Stories Baptisms Engagement in Courses

Groups Feel Like Brotherhood

We’re winning when every man knows he’s not walking alone.

  • Tables are safe, consistent, and relational — not lecture halls.
  • Every man feels noticed, named, and known.
  • Group nights are marked by vulnerability, laughter, and purpose.
  • Men take care of each other — practically and spiritually.
How We Measure It
Group Retention Attendance Qualitative Feedback
5

The Movement Is Spreading

We’re winning when Courageous doesn’t stay in our buildings—it spreads to our city and beyond.

  • Men are launching groups in gyms, workplaces, sports leagues, and neighborhoods.
  • Other churches are learning from what God’s doing at GFC.
  • The playbook is being used nationally to equip men’s leaders.
  • Social and digital reach is multiplying the message.

The Culture Feels Right

We’re winning when the tone of our ministry feels like Jesus—humble, hungry, and hustling.

  • Leaders are humble, hungry, and hustle.
  • There’s laughter, brotherhood, and mission clarity.
  • Wins are celebrated, not hoarded.
  • There’s unity among campuses — not comparison.
